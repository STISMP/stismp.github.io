Season One Rules & Guidelines

1. 3DAYS PEACEFUL: No PvP or killing someone for the first 3 days
2. No Griefing: Don't destroy or steal other players' builds or items. Respect player builds
3. Be Respectful: Treat everyone kindly. No bullying
4. PvP Limitations: Be respectful during Player vs. Player combat and do not attack players in protected areas like spawn points
5. No Hacking or Cheating: Mods or clients that give unfair advantages are not allowed
6. No Spamming or Advertising: Keep the chat clean; don't flood it or promote other servers
7. No Stealing: Take only what you've earned. Respect other players' chests and property
8. Follow Staff Instructions: Admins and moderators have the final say
9. DON'T START END PORTAL WAIT OTHERS!!

Server Rules Enforcement Notice

If you disobey the rules, be prepared for consequences.

  • You may face warnings, temporary bans, or even a permanent ban, depending on the offense.
  • Repeated rule-breaking might also result in item removal, build rollbacks, or muted chat privileges.
  • And if you really push it... the Admin Hammer of Justice will strike — swiftly and without mercy! ⚠️
